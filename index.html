About Us

Friendz Pizzeria is a funky, fun, hometown restaurant. We offer a laid back atmosphere where you can always find music playing and sports on the TV. All of our dough is made right here in the restaurant. We also serve gluten free pizzas! While we specialize in pizza, we also offer lots of different options including appetizers, sandwiches, salads and more. Our full bar offers a wide variety of wine, beer and mixed drinks. Stop by for some great food and a fun time!
